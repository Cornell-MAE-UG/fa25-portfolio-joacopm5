# Client Pitch

**Team:** _Big Red Stompers_

## Problem Statement
Spotted lanternflies (SLF) target grapevines, causing rapid population growth from egg masses containing 30-60 eggs.

## Impact
Attacking the root cause protects regions from losses between 1.5 million and 8.8 million dollars.
